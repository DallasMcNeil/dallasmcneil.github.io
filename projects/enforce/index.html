ENFORCE is an abstract physics puzzle game where an Orb is moved to an End Point using Force Fields. Manipulate Fields to navigate the Orb around countless obstacles and challenges in 60 demanding levels.Try solutions and adjust Fields to create interesting and unique results to complete challenging objectives.

60 difficult Levels with interesting and varying gameplay

Multiple challenges per level with unique solutions

Colourful and stylised graphics with a progressive electronic soundtrack

Record and share your solutions with friends on iOS

Game Center Achievements and challenges

Note: ENFORCE is not longer available on iOS.
